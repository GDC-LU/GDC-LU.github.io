Les Pimpampel ASBL - 5 July 2015

C.H.L Children’s Clinic Volunteer Association

The “Pimpampel a.s.b.l.” charity was established in 1998 and is run by volunteers who organize daily fun and cultural activities, along with offering support and and care within The Children’s Clinic (“Kannerklinik”) of the C.H.L.

The charity’s financial resources come mostly from donations and miscellaneous gifts, on top of the proceeds of events, such as the Christmas Market. The funds are used to pay for specific installations, toys and games or whichever items to make the hospitalization of sick children better.
